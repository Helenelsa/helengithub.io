About Amity University

Amity University is dedicated to providing world-class education and fostering a vibrant campus life.

Our mission is to create a learning environment that nurtures innovation, leadership, and academic excellence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide education that transforms lives and creates responsible global citizens. We aim to equip our students with the knowledge, skills, and values required to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

Our Vision

Amity University envisions being a leading institution recognized globally for excellence in education, research, and innovation. We aspire to create a community of lifelong learners committed to making a positive impact on society.

Our Values

At Amity University, we uphold the following core values:

  • Integrity: We act with honesty and integrity in all our endeavors.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in education, research, and service.
  • Innovation: We foster a culture of innovation and creativity.
  • Diversity: We embrace diversity and inclusiveness in our community.
  • Collaboration: We promote collaboration and teamwork across disciplines.
  • Responsibility: We encourage social responsibility and ethical behavior.
